# Land Clearing Services for New Construction in Dearborn County

Planning a new build on undeveloped land? Probst Excavating specializes in professional land clearing services throughout Aurora, Lawrenceburg, and Dearborn County. With over 20 years of local experience, we transform raw land into ready-to-build sites.

## What is Land Clearing?

Land clearing is the process of removing trees, brush, stumps, rocks, and other obstacles from a property to prepare it for construction, farming, or other development. It's the essential first step in any building project.

## Our Land Clearing Services

### Tree and Vegetation Removal
- Safe removal of trees of all sizes
- Brush and undergrowth clearing
- Selective clearing to preserve desired trees
- Complete site clearing for maximum usable space

### Stump Removal and Grinding
- Complete stump extraction
- Below-grade grinding
- Root removal to prevent future issues
- Site cleanup and debris removal

### Debris Management
- On-site grinding and mulching
- Hauling away unwanted materials
- Environmentally responsible disposal
- Recycling wood and organic materials when possible
